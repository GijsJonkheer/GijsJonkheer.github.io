Creativity

Creative Problem Solving:

The foremost reason for choosing product design as my intended carreer path is it allows me to focus on solving problems creatively. I do also have experience with optimilization, however I can't help but notice that creative solutions are ofthen far more effective and efficient.

In my first year at university I got the assignment to design a playground for a neighbouring town that had something to do with water. I set myself the goal to use the water analogy for electricity to let children learn the basic working principles of a computer. By intergrating "logic ports" with the regular system of letting children build their own water routes, the children are able to "program" the playground.
